How to Use

FieldEstimate lets you photograph a job site, organise photos by room, and generate a cost estimate — all from your phone.

1
Create a New Job
Tap the ＋ button in the bottom right corner. Enter the job name or address, then tap Create. Your new job will appear on the Jobs screen.
2
Add Photos
Open the job you just created and tap the 📷 button to add photos. Photograph every area that needs repair or attention — there's no limit. The more you capture, the more accurate your estimate will be.
3
Sort Photos by Room or Area
Assign photos to specific rooms (e.g. Kitchen, Bedroom, Bathroom):
  1. Tap each photo that belongs to one area to select it
  2. An Assign to Room → button appears at the bottom
  3. Tap it, then choose an existing room or create a new one
  4. Repeat for each group of photos until every photo is assigned
4
Analyse by Room
Once photos are sorted, the Rooms tab will appear at the top. Tap it to see your room list. Each room has an ⚡ Analyse Room button — tap it to generate a cost estimate for that area.
5
Get the Full Project Total
Tap 📊 Analyse Project to see the estimate for the entire job — broken down by room and trade. Every line item can be edited directly to adjust figures and match your budget.
💡 Pro tip: Label your photos before analysing — it helps the AI understand what each area is and gives you a more accurate scope of works.
💡 Pro version unlocks the full materials breakdown, labour table editing, custom material prices, and PDF export. Upgrade in Settings.
